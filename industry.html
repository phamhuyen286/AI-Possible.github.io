Industry Applications

Composite AI - a Near Future Possibility

AI is revolutionizing oil & gas with:

  • Predictive Maintenance: AI monitors rigs & pipelines via IoT to prevent failures (LSTM, Transformers).
  • Seismic & Reservoir Analysis: AI detects oil reserves from seismic data (CNNs – UNet, ResNet).
  • Real-time Drilling Optimization: AI adjusts drilling parameters for efficiency (Reinforcement Learning).
  • Supply Chain Optimization: AI predicts oil demand & extracts insights from reports (LLMs).

Composite AI enhances accuracy by combining:

  • Hybrid Models: LSTM, Transformers & XGBoost for forecasting.
  • Knowledge Graphs: Industry data for deeper insights.
  • Physics-Informed AI: Engineering simulations for better maintenance.
